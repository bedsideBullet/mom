About Me

Hello, I'm Rebecca Smith, your friendly neighborhood notary public serving Springtown, Texas, and nearby areas. With a passion for providing dependable and personalized notary services, I am dedicated to meeting the diverse needs of my community with professionalism and integrity.

As a solo practitioner, I offer a hands-on approach to notarization, ensuring that each client receives the individual attention and care they deserve. Whether you require assistance with real estate transactions, legal documents, or personal paperwork, I am here to assist you every step of the way.

With years of experience in the field, I understand the importance of accuracy and confidentiality in every notarial act. You can trust me to handle your documents with the utmost care and attention to detail, ensuring that they are properly notarized and legally binding.

As a Springtown resident myself, I am deeply invested in the well-being of our community. I strive to make the notarization process as convenient and stress-free as possible for my clients, offering flexible appointment times and mobile notary services for those unable to visit my office.

Whether you're a homeowner, business owner, or individual in need of notary services, I am here to help. Contact me today to schedule an appointment or learn more about how I can assist you with your notarial needs.

Top Services Offered

Acknowledgement

An acknowledgment is a formal declaration before an authorized official such as a notary public, by a person who has executed an instrument, that the execution was voluntary and authentic.

Notarization of Legal Documents

Notaries in Texas are authorized to notarize various legal documents including affidavits, deeds, contracts, powers of attorney, and more.

Mobile Notary Services

Mobile notaries are available to travel to a client's location, such as a home, office, hospital, or other convenient location, to perform notarial acts.
